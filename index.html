Facial Recognition Access

Mrdocs secures your documents with facial recognition, only granting access when it confirms your identity, ensuring privacy and security.

Liveness Check

Before signing, Mrdocs requires users to perform gestures to confirm their presence, enhancing the authenticity of the biometric signature.

QR Code Verification

Each signed document generates a unique QR code linked to the document ID, allowing verification of signees and preventing tampering.

Document Upload

Users can upload documents by browsing or scanning directly within the app for easy access and signing.

Face-Based Signing

Unlike traditional e-signatures, Mrdocs captures a real-time selfie for each signature, authenticating signers through facial recognition.

Signees List

Scan a document’s QR code to view all signees, including their names, photos, and signature details, ensuring transparency and traceability.

SIGN NDA DOCUMENTS

Your innovative idea is now secure with MrDocs. With our Biometric Signature app, your audience can confidently trust that their documents are protected.

Identity Verification Requests

Credit providers can verify a user’s identity by requesting a Mrdocs-signed document to confirm authenticity.

Anti-Impersonation Measures

Mrdocs helps prevent identity theft by notifying users when their documents are used, stopping fraud in real time.

Facial Expression Detection

To prevent forced use, Mrdocs stops transactions if the app detects unusual expressions like sadness or distress.

Request and Decline Control

Users can view, accept, or decline document requests, giving them full control over their personal data and preventing misuse.

Global Money Transfer

FlexiWallet enables users to send and receive money internationally, providing convenient financial access.

Online Payments with Facial Approval

For every transaction, Mrdocs requires facial verification to confirm the user’s identity, enhancing transaction security.

Digital Wallet Security

FlexiWallet acts as a digital wallet within Mrdocs, where funds can be stored securely and accessed only with biometric approval.

Anti-Coercion Technology

FlexiWallet detects user distress through facial expressions, terminating transactions if the user appears under duress.

How does FlexiWallet Work

FlexiWallet allows users to make secure payments without needing to enter card details. Using only facial approval, you can send and receive South African rands internationally.

Document Submission for Loan Approval

Mrdocs simplifies loan applications by allowing users to upload required documents like payslips and bank statements, with biometric verification.

Direct Partner Applications

Users can apply to partnered credit providers directly within Mrdocs, streamlining the application and verification process.

Automatic Credit Analysis

Using AI, Mrdocs analyzes financial documents and shares data insights. It also retrieves the applicant’s credit score via API integration for quick assessment.

Custom API for Credit Providers

Mrdocs offers an API for organizations to integrate identity verification, document request, and loan processing into their own platforms.

How does this work?

Mrdocs enables users to apply for loans from partnered credit providers by uploading and securely signing necessary documents with facial recognition, reducing identity theft fraud.
